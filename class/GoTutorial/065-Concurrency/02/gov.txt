<section class="local-government">
<h2 class="home-heading small">Local government</h2>
<p>
The 6 states and the Northern Territory have established 1 further
level of government. Local governments (also known as city, shire, town or
municipal councils) handle community needs like waste collection, public
recreation facilities and town planning.
</p>
<p>
The
<strong>
<a href="http://alga.asn.au/">Australian Local Government Association</a>
</strong>
is the national voice of local government, representing 560 councils
across the country. State local government directories provide links to
local council sites:
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://www.olg.nsw.gov.au/local-government-directory">New South Wales local government directory</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="https://dlgcs.nt.gov.au/">Northern Territory local government directory</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="http://www.dilgp.qld.gov.au/local-government-directory/">Queensland local government directory</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="http://www.lga.sa.gov.au/councils">South Australia local government directory</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="http://www.dpac.tas.gov.au/divisions/local_government/local_government_directory">Tasmania local government directory</a>
</li>

</li>
<li>
